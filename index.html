A FABTOTUM PERSONAL FABRICATOR CAN…

3D PRINT

3D Prints all materials at High Resolution: the FABtotum PRO opens up to an endless number of ideas and projects. Find out more about Printing Head and Printing Head PRO.

  • 3D Prints all materials (PLA, ABS, TPU etc)
  • Interchangeable Nozzles
  • Heated Printing Area
  • Made In Italy Design

CNC MILL

You can cut and engrave using the Milling Head and the FABtotum PRO. Work many different materials like Wood, Aluminum, Brass and Copper. Make Molds, Finished parts or PCB prototypes.

  • 200W Brushless motor
  • Cuts woods, foams, metals etc.
  • Engraves all surfaces, PCBs included
  • 14000 RPM Max

LASER ENGRAVE & CUT

From 3D Printer to Laser Engraver in a few seconds: with Laser Head ora Laser Head PRO a FABtotum PRO can help the personalization of products, whether it is a prototype or a personal object.

  • Writes on all non-reflective surfaces
  • Cuts Paper and Cardboard
  • Etches PCBs
  • Safe for Home use
